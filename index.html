About Me

Krishnakumar AP is a researcher in Meteorology with expertise in atmospheric aerosols, cloud interactions, and climate data analysis. With a background in weather forecasting and satellite remote sensing, he has worked with institutions like the National Centre for Medium Range Weather Forecasting (NCMRWF) and has conducted graduate research in the United States focusing on environmental data applications.

His passion lies in making atmospheric data more accessible, visual, and research-friendly through tools like this data navigator. This site is a step toward democratizing meteorological data access for students, scientists, and enthusiasts worldwide.
