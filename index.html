About Me

Krishnakumar AP is a researcher in Meteorology with expertise in atmospheric aerosols especially in dust aerosols, cloud interactions, and climate data analysis. With a background in weather forecasting and satellite remote sensing, he has worked with different institutions.

His passion lies in making atmospheric data more accessible, visual, and research-friendly through tools like this data archive wizard. This site is a step toward democratizing meteorological data access for students, scientists, and enthusiasts worldwide.
